Fermentation Process

From Soak To Cellar

Our terroir begins where the Oakland soil, old concrete, rusted access ladders, street runoff mythology, and the bather microbiome meet in a warm vessel of regret.

Marination. Fourteen explorers enter the tub. Heat encourages disclosure, skin oil release, and collective loss of plausible deniability.
Extraction. Gentle agitation liberates sunscreen film, denim lint, elbow memory, and the emotional sediment of the evening.
Urban resting. The vintage is allowed to contemplate storm drains, side quests, and the long spiritual tradition of going somewhere you were not invited.
Bottling. The final liquid is treated as an art object, cursed sample, and fake wine, never as a beverage.
